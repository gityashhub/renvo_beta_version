## Objective

RenvoAI should become an AI Agent that can intelligently analyze, plan, recommend, and execute data cleaning tasks, but it must NEVER take irreversible actions without explicit human approval.

Think of it as:

- AI does the thinking
- Human does the approving
- AI executes only after approval

This is Human-in-the-Loop (HITL), not full automation.
